    Research on High Gain Omnidirectional Antenna                    2012.06-2013.04

    Research Assistant  

    Researched two new antenna structures of C-band with high gain and wide band: CPW (Coplanar Waveguide) cross-coupling feed antenna and direct feed antenna;

    Participated in simulations and optimization by CST Microwave Studio for CPW cross-coupling feed antenna; completed the direct feed antenna simulation independently;

    Completed the fabricated antenna and processed the electrical properties such as antenna radiation unit length, oscillator arm-wide factor and radiation element space; tested the real results to compare with simulation results.

    Simulation and Implementation on Printed Slit Antenna                    2012.05-2013.04

    Research Assistant

    Studied the technology of Substrate Integrated Waveguide (SIW) and researched a novel Half-mode Double-ridge Substrate Integrated Waveguide （HMDRSIM）with Balanced Microstrip Transition by CST Microwave Studio;

    Took part in designing Ridge Substrate Integrated Waveguide (RSIW) for the implement of printed circuit board (PCB) and low temperature co-fired ceramic (LTCC);

    Used CST Microwave Studio to simulate the feed waveguide and obtain scattering parameters curve; Tested the real antenna compared with simulation model, including waveguide S parameter, antenna reflection parameters, impedance bandwidth, radiation patterns and gain.
